Training of Paralympic Basketball Players

The training of Paralympic Athletes is a testament to their will and the power and glory of the human body.

  • Paralympic wheelchair basketball athletes train to improve their upper-body strength, speed, and agility
  • Short, intense exercise bouts that include rapid acceleration and deceleration, dynamic position changes, and maintaining or obtaining one's position on the court.
  • Alongside intense agility training, the athletes also improve their Basketball skills by performing drills using various methods.
  • The athletes have to train their core heavily and strengthen it to be able to maneuver their wheelchairs properly.
